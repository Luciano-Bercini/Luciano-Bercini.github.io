▸ ABOUT ME

One-man studio

Hi, I'm Luciano, a passionate indie game developer and the solo force behind the Arcadium series. From the first line of C# to the store screenshots, every piece of these games passes through my hands.

I've been shipping games since 2021, evolving a simple arcade shooter into a full-blown roguelite odyssey across three platforms. I care about tight game feel, readable pixel chaos, and the kind of "one more run" loop that keeps players up too late.

Whether you're a fellow developer, a player, or just curious about the magic behind the pixels: welcome aboard.

Unity C# Visual Studio Git Steamworks App Store Connect Google Play Console

Engine & Code

Unity + C#: gameplay systems, custom tooling, event-driven architecture and object pooling for bullet-heaven-scale performance.

Game Design

Roguelite builds, talent trees, boss design and difficulty curves tuned for both quick sessions and hardcore runs.

Cross-Platform

One codebase shipped to iOS, Android and Steam; touch, mouse and gamepad friendly.

Community

An active Discord where players shape balance patches, test betas and share high scores.
