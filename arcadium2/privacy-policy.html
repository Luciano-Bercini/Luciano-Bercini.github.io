Privacy Policy of "Arcadium - Space War"

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Arcadium - Space War for mobile devices that was created by ihgyug. The application is a mobile arcade game, in retro style, that features a modern and innovative arcade gameplay combined with modern gameplay systems.

User Provided Information

Registration and log-in through Google Play Services (Android) and Game Center (iOS) is optional. However, please keep in mind that you may not be able to use some of the features (achievements, leaderboards, data sync) offered by the Application unless you register.

Automatically Collected Information

The game is powered by the Unity game engine (that is, made with Unity), and as such, the application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, your mobile operating system, country of install, and information about the way you use the application. For more information, you can follow the link: Unity Privacy Policy

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

What are my opt-out rights?

- You can stop all collection of information by the application by going through Options -> Open Data Privacy Page -> Opt-Out and Delete Data.

- You can stop all collection of information by uninstalling the application.
